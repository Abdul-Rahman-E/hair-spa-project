Hair Day: A Stylish Tale in Madison, Wisconsin

In the heart of Madison, Wisconsin, the story of Hair Day unfolds—a tale spun with passion, creativity, and a touch of artistry that breathes life into hair and beauty. Founded by the visionary Pria, this boutique salon is a haven for those seeking more than just a haircut—it's an experience, a journey into self-expression.

Pria, a certified colorist with a fervor for avant-garde coloring techniques, envisioned a space where clients could indulge in a "treat yourself" atmosphere. Alongside her dear friend and masterful hairdresser, Garry, they crafted Hair Day. Their shared commitment to excellence and innovation birthed a space where style met passion.

This salon isn't just a place for cuts, colors, and styles—it's an artistic ensemble offered by a team of seven dedicated professionals. With two seasoned stylists, three skilled colorists, a meticulous manicurist, and a talented makeup artist, Hair Day offers a comprehensive range of beauty services.

What sets Hair Day apart is its signature prowess in edgy hair colors and the latest cutting-edge trends. It's not merely a salon—it's an intimate sanctuary where clients can relax and revel in the transformation of their appearance.

However, such is the demand for their sought-after services that the salon can only accommodate a limited number of coloring appointments each week. To secure a spot, clients are encouraged to plan well in advance for their desired transformation.

But the connection doesn't end at the salon chair. Hair Day believes in nurturing this bond beyond appointments. Through their website, the team shares makeup and nail tutorials, empowering clients to maintain their style and beauty between visits.

Hair Day isn't just about hairstyles or nail art—it's about fostering an identity, creating a canvas for self-expression, and inspiring confidence. It's about transcending trends to unveil a unique, authentic you.

Join the narrative at Hair Day, where every visit becomes an integral chapter in your story. Book an appointment, step into our warm and inviting space, and let us weave magic into your personal style journey.
